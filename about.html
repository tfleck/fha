About the Camp


The camp was started in 2009 by Chris Nagele in the wake of the closure of Treasure Island
The camp started out as a pioneering camp with a handful of really passionate guys
It has since grown to include a trail crew program, and what used to be Resica's High 5 program

"I can't bring back the Island, but I hope to keep its spirit alive here"
- Chris Nagele, Camp Director
